Menu Controls:
W and S to move
Space to select 

Build Stage Controls:
Rotate cards using A and D
Mouse 1 to pick up cards
Cards have WASD meaning which key will activate them 


How to play:
Bring cards below the line to reveal them. Placing cards below the line locks them in place, placing cards above the line doesn't.
Rotate cards using A and D

There are 3 kind of cards, engine, gun and weight. 
Engine and gun generates thrust in the direction which they are pointed to.
Weight doesn't generate thrust, it only moves the center of mass around which is indicated with a red square.
